Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk is an engineer, industrial designer, and technology entrepreneur. He is a citizen of South Africa, Canada and the United States, and is the founder, CEO and chief engineer/designer of SpaceX; co-founder, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink; and co-founder and initial co-chairman of OpenAI
